What is inside of cells?

Animal Cells:

  • Cell membrane- encloses a cell, acts as a barrier and decides what can or can’t enter the cell.
  • Cytoplasm- jelly-like substance that holds all of the organelles.
  • Ribosomes- proteins that float around in the cell.
  • Golgi complex- packages proteins in vesicles
  • Nucleus- holds DNA for cell
  • Endoplasmic Reticulum- gets rid of harmful materials in cells.
  • Vacuole- stores nutrients for cell
  • Mitochondria- provides energy for the cell to function

  • Cell wall- gives structure to the plant body
