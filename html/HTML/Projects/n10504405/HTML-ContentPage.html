What is RGB and why is it important to computers?

RGB stands for Red Green Blue, and is used on lights in a computer to light it up! Here you can see that the lights are all around the window to make the glass light up!

RGB is used in all sorts of cool and fun ways, like lighting up the inside of a computer or the outside on your desk! This computer desk has lights on the underside of it.

You can make really cool computers with this awesome lighting. This one has an RGB keyboard and mouse too!
