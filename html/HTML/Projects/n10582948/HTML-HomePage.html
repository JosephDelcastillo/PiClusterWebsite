  • Color Wheel: is an abstract illustrative organization of color hues around a circle which shows the relationships between primary colors, secondary colors, tertiary colors etc.
    • Primary Colors: Colors at their basic essence; those colors that cannot be created by mixing others. Secondary Colors: Those colors are achieved by a mixture of two primary colors.
    Tertiary Colors: Those colors are achieved by a mixture of primary and secondary hues. Complementary Colors: Those colors are located on the opposite from each other on the color wheel. Analogous Colors: These colors are also located close together on the color wheel.
