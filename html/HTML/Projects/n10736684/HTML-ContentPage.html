Cells, Tissues, and Organs

Cell Theory


1. All living organisms are composed of one or more cells.
2. The cell is the basic unit of structure and organization in organisms.
3. Cells arise from pre-existing cells.

Tissue


A combination of cells form what is called tissue, these tissues work together to perform specific functions, like eating an apple.

Organs


Formed by tissue that performs an important vital function for your body, like the heart and brain.
