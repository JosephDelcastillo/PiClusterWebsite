Mars
Mars, alos known as the Red Planet is the 4th planet from the sun and the second smallest in size. A day takes a little 24 hours, while the year takes 687 Earth days. Right now, Mars can't support life, but there are missions that are trying to determine Mars's past and future.

Did You Know?
  • It has a thin atmopshpere
  • It has two moons
  • It has seasons like Earth
  • It's the most explored planet
