Venus
Venus is the second planet from the Sun and the third smallest planet in the solar system. It's very similar to Earth, but it's covered in volcanoes and has acidic clouds. Venus is the hottest planet, reaching 900 degrees Fahrenheit, enough to melt lead! Life on Venus is probably not possible because of it's environmental conditions.

Did You Know?
  • It spins in the opposite direction
  • A day lasts longer than it's year, at 243 Earth days
  • It has no rings nor moons
  • Because it rotates so slowly, winds blow at hurricane forces
