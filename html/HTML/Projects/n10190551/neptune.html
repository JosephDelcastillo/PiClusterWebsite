Neptune
Neptune is the 8th planet and the most distant from the sun and the 4th largest planet in the solar system. The ice giant is made with helium, hydrogen, and methane, and is very dark, cold, and has supersonic winds, up to 1,200 mph! A day takes 16 hours while it takes 165 earth years to orbit the sun. As it's the most distant planet, it's the only one not visible to the naked eye.

Did You Know?
  • It has 13 moons and faint rings
  • Voyager 2 was the only spacecraft to visit Neptune
  • Due to Pluto's orbit, sometimes Pluto is closeer to us than Neptune is
  • Neptune's moon, Triton, is the only moon that's opposite of Neptune's direction
