Metamorphic Rock

A metamorphic rock is created when former igneous or sedimentary rocks undergo high levels of pressure and heat.

Sedimentary Rock

Sedimentary rocks are formed when bits and pieces of all rocks become sediments and are compacted.

Igneous Rock

Igneous rocks form after metamorphic rocks are heated so much that it turns into lava. When the lava cools down it turns into igneous rocks.
