What are Volcanoes?
A Volcano is a vent in the planet's crust that spews out lava, ash, rock and gases.
Volcanoes are usually found where tectonic plates are diverging or converging, and others are found underwater.
Volcanoes has been on earth way before we have existed. They were on earth even before the Dinosaurs!
