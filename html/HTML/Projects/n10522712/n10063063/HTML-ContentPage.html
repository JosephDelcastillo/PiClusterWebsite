What is a video game

A game played by electronically manipulating images produced by a computer program on a television screen or other display screen.

Types of video games

• Action • Platform games• Action-adventure• Adventure• Role-playing• Action RPG• Simulation• Construction and management simulation• Strategy• Sports• Racing• MMO

How video games are helpful

Playing video games can improve reaction times and hand-eye co-ordination. For example,research has shown that visualization ability, such as mentally rotating and manipulating two- and three-dimensional objects, improves with video game playing. This can improve the child reaction time in life.
