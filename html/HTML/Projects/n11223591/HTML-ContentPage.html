Photosynthesis is...

Photosynthesis is a process used by plants and other organisms to convert light energy, normally from the Sun, into chemical energy that can be later released to fuel the organisms' activities (energy transformation).

This picture demonstrates the basic process of photosynthesis

The Steps of Photosynthesis

  1. The chlorophyll traps sunlight in the leaves
  2. The sunlight gives energy to the plant to start making food
  3. The roots suck nutrients to the leaves
  4. The leaves mix carbon dioxide, the nutrients and water to make sugar
  5. The plants throw out waste, or oxygen
