Facts
The first known robot was made in the 5th century BC by Archytas of Tarentum (Philosopher) which was a long time ago. He made mechanical doves driven by a jet of steam which isn't a big deal but is still the first robot ever made.
“Robot” comes from the Czech word robota, meaning“drudgery,” and first appeared in the 1921 play R.U.R. (Rossum’s Universal Robots). So people did not come up with the name until almost 100 years ago. But people were making robots before that.
There are multiple different kinds of robots that can be used for different things. Robots are mainly used to do the jobs that a human would have to do. They are programmed to do what is in the code. Some of the different kinds of robots include, house cleaning robots, factory robots, medical robots, space robots.
