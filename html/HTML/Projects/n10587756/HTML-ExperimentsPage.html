How did the Moon get its Craters?

To start this experiment, you're going to need these materials:

  • Magic mud
  • safety goggles
  • lab coat (optional)
  • Rocks (optional)

Instructions

  1. First put on your safety equipment, then form the magic mud into a ball
  2. Then use rocks or your fingers to impale the ball of magic mud
  3. This is similar to how meteors crashed into the moon's surface making its craters
  4. Celebrate! now that you know how the moon's craters were made
