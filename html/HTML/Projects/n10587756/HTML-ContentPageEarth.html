Earth:

Did you know?

  1. Earth is the only Planet in the solar system not named after a Greek or Roman deity.
  2. Earth is the only known planet to support life.
  3. Earth is not a perfect sphere
  4. Earth is about 4.54 billion years old.
  5. If you’re reading this, you probably live on Earth!

Get to know Earth:

  • Mass: 5.972 × 10^24 kilometers
  • Volume: 1 trillion cubic kilometers
  • Surface area: 510 million square kilometers
  • Equatorial circumference: 40,070 kilometers
  • Polar circumference (meridional circumference): 39,931 kilometers
  • Density: 5.513 grams per cubic centimeter
  • Equatorial radius: 6,378 kilometers
  • Polar Radius: 6,356 kilometers
