Egypt
Both Egyptian men and women wore make-up. The eye-paint was usually green. Egyptians believed make-up had magical healing powers, too!
The Ancient Egyptians invented lots of things we still use today, such as paper, pens, locks and keys and – believe it or not – toothpaste!
  • The Egyptians had pets like cats, dogs, monkeys, and even a crocodile but they weren't friendly animals.
  • The Nile River is the biggest river that goes through Egypt. The river is 4,258 miles long!
  • Heliopolis is the oldest city in Egypt
