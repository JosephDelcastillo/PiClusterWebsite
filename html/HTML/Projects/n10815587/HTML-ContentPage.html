The Asteroid Belt

The Asteroid Belt is located between Mars and Jupiter, think of it as a seperation between the inner and outer planets. The Asteroid belt is where most, if not all, the asteroids in our solar system are located. Also, some dwarf planets can be found in the asteroid belt.
