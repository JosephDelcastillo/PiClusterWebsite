Volcanoes
• A volcano is a landform where molten rock erupts through the surface of the planet • A volcano has a mountain type of shape with an open top • The volcano is filled with magma • Magma is a liquid rock • Lava is the liquid rock that flows out of a volcano • Fresh lava ranges from 1,300 degrees to 2,200 degrees • The tectonic plates cause earthquakes which makes the volcano to erupt its magma • The Ring of Fire is over half of the world’s volcanoes arise in a belt around the Pacific Ocean
Volcano project Materials
• 10 ml of dish soap • 100 ml of cold water • 400 ml of white vinegar • Food coloring • Baking soda slurry (fill a cup about ½ with baking soda, then fill the rest of the way with water) • Empty 2-liter soda bottle
Instructions
1. Combine the vinegar, water, dish soap and 2 drops of food coloring into the empty soda bottle. 2. Use spoon to mix the baking soda slurry it is all a liquid 3. Eruption time.... Pour the baking soda slurry into the soda bottle quickly and step back!
