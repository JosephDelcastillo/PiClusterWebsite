Page Overview
This page is here to benefit you in your process of learning about astronomy. You will learn all the different standards you need to know for 5th grade astronomy.
What is Astronomy?
Astronomy is a natural science that studies the celestial objects(planet, stars, etc.) and phenomena that occur in space.
History of Astronomy
Astronomy is one of the world's oldest natural sciences. Earliest forms of astronomy can be linked to the early civilizations like the Babylonians, Greeks, Indians, Egyptians, Nubians, Iranians, Chinese, Maya, and many ancient indigenous peoples of the Americas.
