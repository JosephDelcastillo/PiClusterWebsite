Revolutionary war

Causes for the Revolutionary war

  • 7 years war also known as the French Indian war.
  • Treaty of Paris didn't allow the Colonist to move on to Indian land.
  • The war debt was paid using taxes from the colonies.
  • Colonist were upset because they had no say in these taxes
  • Stamp act of 1765. This made it so all paper had to have a special stamp on it and this stamp was not free.
  • The Sons of Liberty helped get boycotts which made it so the British government had to take away the tax.
  • One boycott got out of hand. It was called the Boston Massacre. 5 colonist died in this. No British soldiers were severely punished for this
  • The Tea act made the colonist more made and they had what was called the Boston Tea Party. A bunch of Sons of liberty got dressed up as Indians and dumped 4 million dollars worth of tea into the water.
  • Then came the Massachusetts government act which restricted self government.
  • The quartering act forced colonist to house British soldiers in there home
  • The Quebec act lowered the Quebec boundary into colonist land, and it came with religious toleration which meant all regions were allowed.
  • People started talking of natural rights. Then fighting started and that started the war.

The War

The first real fighting started in Lexington and Concord. Where minute men (which were our first real soldiers) fought British soldiers. Militia chased the British back to Boston from concord. then the Continental Congress ordered the MIlitia to be put into a Continental army Lead by General George Washington. The American lost Charles Town to the British in the Battle of Bunker hill. British hired soldiers from different countries to come help them in the fighting. General Washington put cannon pointing at Boston this made the British flee for Halifax in Canada. General Washington moved his forces to new York and they were meet with more British troops. 1776 Thomas Jefferson wrote the Declaration of Independence and sent it to the king. this said the the 13 colonies were no longer colonies but their own nation, the USA. George Washington and his troops were eventually ran out of New York by the British. Washington's troops escapes across the Delaware river to Pennsylvania. British didn't follow them there because of the winter coming. On Christmas Washington crossed the Delaware river with his troops to attack the British forces in Trenton. this lead to a few more American victories. in 1777 British forces from Quebec pushed down to take the new england area. this was ultimately stopped at Saratoga. also in 1777 Congress official got the American flag. in 1778 France came into the war and officially was with he Americans and sent ships over to help us. British started arming Native Americans and loyalist. Washington sent troops on the Sullivan Expedition to burn down there villages and crops. In 1779 Spain entered the war on the American side. In 1780 British forces captured Charleston. Cornwallis was left in charge of the southern troop. American forced were defeated at Camden sop Britain got Georgia and South Carolina. Cornwallis tried to move into North Carolina was General Nathan Greene stopped him. By the end of 1781 the British only controlled 3 cities in the south Savannah, Charleston, and Wilmington. Cornwallis continued north and got Yorktown. French ships moved into and got rid of British ships there cutting off Cornwallis supplies. Troops came in from the other side and captured Cornwallis. the treaty of Paris of 1783 officially ended the war.
