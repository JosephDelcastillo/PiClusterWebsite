Barcelona, Spain
Sports- There's only one really famous sport that is played in Barcelona that trumps the rest and that is Futbol/Soccer. There is a team in Barcelona, called FCBarcelona. FCBarcelona is the most successful team in history with 94 trophies since 1899.
Government- Spain is a constitutional monarchy whose government is defined by the Constitution of Spain. The Government of Spain is the central governemnt which leads the executive branch and the General Administration of Spain.
  • The cultures of Spain are European cultures based on a variety of historical influences
  • By the end of the 19th and 20th centuries, the Spaniards made expressions of cultural diversity easier than it had been for the last seven centuries.
  • The term "Spanish literature" refers to literature written in the Spanish language, including literature composed by Spanish and Latin American writers.
