What is Evolution? by Stated Clearly

VIDEO QUIZ QUESTIONS

  1. True or False: Evolution does not occur in modern creatures.

  2. What determines how organisms look and behave?
    1. DNA
    2. RNA
    3. mRNA

  3. What is evolution?
    1. The branch of science concerned with fossil animals and plants.
    2. A cycle of events in time or a recurring period of time.
    3. Any change in the heritable traits within a population across generations.
    4. A self-replicating material present in nearly all living organisms as the carrier of genetic information.

  4. True or False: All organisms are related to a common ancestor.

  5. What natural process can function as a breeder?
    1. Meiosis
    2. Natural Selection
    3. Paleontology
    4. DNA

ANSWERS

  1. False
  2. A. DNA
  3. C. Any change in the heritable traits within a population across generations.
  4. True
  5. B. Natural Selection
