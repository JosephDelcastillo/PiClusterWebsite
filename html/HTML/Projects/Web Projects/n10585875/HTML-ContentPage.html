Materials for making a volcano.
You will need an empty plastic bottle (this is 1 litre bottle), newspaper torn up in strips, PVA craft glue, masking tape and a container for the water and glue mixture. To make the lava to mimic that of a real volcano you will need bicarbonate soda, red and yellow food colouring (or orange) and white vinegar.

Basic instructions for making a Volcano!
  1. Lay down a sheet of wax paper.
  2. Place a container at the center.
  3. Use clay to make the rest of the volcano.
  4. Leave for one hour or until dry.
  5. Mix your vinegar.
  6. Pour the mixture into the volcano.
  7. Package your baking soda.
  8. Drop the baking soda roll in the vinegar.
  9. Once the paper dissolves, your volcano will explode.
