Geosphere: The Geosphere includes the Earth's crust, all the way to the core. Which includes all rocks, minerals, mountains, and magma.
Hydrosphere: The Hydrosphere is all liquid water found on the Earth, including Streams, to Lakes, and even to Oceans.
Biosphere: The Biosphere is layers of Earth that allows for life to exist and thrive. Extending from mountain ranges to as much as 8 kilometers or more depths in the ocean.
Atmosphere: The Atmosphere is the gas layers formed around the Earth, with it being composed of the Troposphere, Stratosphere, Mesosphere, and Thermo-sphere, ranging from bottom to top.
