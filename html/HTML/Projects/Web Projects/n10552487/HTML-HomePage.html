Page Overview
This page is intended to expand your knowledge on an elementary level, of the human body and its systems.
Human Biology
Biology is the study of life, human biology is the study of human life and the human body.
Why is it important?
Studying the human body helps us understand how it works, and how to live healthier lives. Learning the different parts of the human body and its many systems can help explain how and why it works.
