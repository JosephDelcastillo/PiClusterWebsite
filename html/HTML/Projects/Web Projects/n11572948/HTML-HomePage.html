What was World War I?
World War I was the global war that lasted from July 28, 1914 to November 11, 1918, and involved 32 countries.
What caused WWI?
The causes of WWI can be broken down into five parts using the acronym MANIA.
What does MANIA stand for?
  • Militarism
  • Alliances
  • Nationalism
  • Imperialism
  • Assassination
