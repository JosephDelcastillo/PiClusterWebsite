Jupiter
Jupiter is the 5th planet from the Sun and the largest planet of the solar system. A day on Juiter takes 10 hours, and 12 years for it to rotate around the sun. The gas giant is made up of hydrogen and helium. It lacks a surface because it's a gas giant, but has a solid inner core. Jupiter has something called the Great Red Spot, which is a storm that's been there since 1830!

Did You Know?
  • It has 69 moons in toal and faint rings
  • It can't support life, but one of it's moons might be able to
  • It could have been a star, but it didn't grow big enough
  • It has the largest ocean (of hydrogen instead of water)
