Ceres
Ceres is a dwarf planet in the Asterioid Belt, and is also the largest object there. It takes 4.5 earth years to orbit the sun, and days take 9 hours. It's very small though, with Pluto being 14 times bigger than it! Ceres has these bright spots dicovered by the NASA spacecraft Dawn that are currently can't be explained.

Did You Know?
  • It became the first dwarf planet to recieve a visit from a spacecraft (Dawn)
  • It takes up 35% of the Asteroid Belt's mass
  • It has no atmopshere, no moons, and no rings
