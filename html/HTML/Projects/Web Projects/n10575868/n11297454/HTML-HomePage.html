Science Experiemts

This website will teach you how to do three science experiments of your choosing. These experiemtns cosist of making slime, foam and crystals!


Slime

An easy experiment you can do with some water, corn starch and food coloring!


Foam

Make a cool foam explosion with only some hydrogen peroxide, yeast and dish soap, and of coarse some food coloring!


Crystals

Make cool colorful crystals from some coal, amonia and salt!

In the Content page you can find all materials needed for the experiments. Your can also find step by step instructions on how to perform the experiments and stay safe. It includes pictures of how the experiemtns will look once performed.

If you go on the Videos page you can view tutorial videos on how to perform the experiments. Each experiment has its own video showing footage of the experiment.
