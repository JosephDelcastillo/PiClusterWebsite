Creating A Volcano!

Material

1. Wax paper
2. Soda can
3. Clay
4. Red food coloring
5. Vinegar
6. Dish soap
7. Toilet Paper
8. Baking Soda
Instructions
1. Lay down a sheet of wax paper
2. Place a container at the center
3. Use clay to make the rest of the volcano
4. Leave for one hour or until dry
5. Mix your Vinegar with the food coloring
6. Pour mixture into the volcano
7. Package your baking soda
8. Drop the baking soda roll in the vinegar
9. Step away
