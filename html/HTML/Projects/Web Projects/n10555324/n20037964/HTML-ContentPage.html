The Main Parts to Weather
What is Weather?

Weather is the state of the atmosphere at any given period of time. The movement of the Earth is what causes wind, clouds, storms, and precipitation. What is precipitation? Precipitaion is moisture that falls to the ground from clouds in the form of rain or snow.

Atmosphere

The atmosphere is a thin layer of gases that surrounds Earth. Its main purpose is to seal the planet and protect us from the radiation given off by the Sun and small objects such as meteoroids. It also holds the oxygen we need to survive. There are layers in the atmosphere similar to inside of the Earth.The thickness of the atmosphere is a balance between the gravity of the Earth and energetic molecules that want to rise and move towards space. If the Earth happened to be larger, the atmosphere would be denser, or thicker.
