Zoologist
A zoologist is an expert in or student of the behavior, physiology, classification, and distribution of animals.
Zoology is a wide field offering many career opportunities for research. A career in zoology offers an opportunity to make a difference to the plant's ecology through conservation work.
All zoology specializations involve work with animals, either in the wild or in a lab. some zoologist study the entire organism while other zoologist study only parts of an organism.
