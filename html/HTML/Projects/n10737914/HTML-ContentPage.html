Why Do We Need Volcano's and Why Do They Erupt
Volcano's are the Earths way of cooling off and releasing hot gases. The reason they erupt is because gas bubbles build up pressure pushing magma toward the surface. This is kinda like when you shake up a soda. First you shake it up and don't notice a difference until you open it and all the gas bubbles push the soda out.
Facts About Volcano's
  • You might think there is only the cone shaped volcano but that is only on type. Other types include a wide plateaus (an area of high ground level) and cracks that release lava on the side of the mountain.

  • Not all volcano's are above the ground some are deep in the oceans or even under icecaps.

  • Volcanic eruptions can send ash as high as 17 miles up!

  • The word volcano comes from the Roman god of fire, Vulcan.
