What Is A Volcano

A volcano is a break in the Earth or any other planets surface. A volcano is formed when huge plate called tectonic plates break the Earths crust.These plates float on a hotter, soft layer called the mantle. When a volcano erupts in shoots hot hot lava, volcanic ash, and gases. These are all stored in the magma chamber which lies below the surface of the Earth.

Diagram of a Volcano
Vocabulary:
Tectonic Plate- A theory that the there are 7 large plates under the Earths Surface the move and rotate.
Crust-The first layer of the Earth that we stand on.
Mantle- The second layer of the Earth that is underneath the crust.
Magma Chamber- A large pool of liquid rock that is below the surface of the Earth.
