Pharaohs and Dynasty's
Most of the Egyptian Pyramids were built for pharaohs and there are a total of 130 discovered pyramids in Egypt.
The 1st dynasty of Egypt started in 2925-2775 BCE (Before Common Era). The Final Dynasty in Ancient Egypt was the Ptolemaic Dynasty which lasted up until 30 BCE
The tallest Pyramid in Egypt is the Great Pyramid of Giza which is 481 feet high. The shortest Pyramid in Egypt being the Pyramid of Menkaure with it being 213 feet tall.
Famous Egyptian Rulers
  • Narmer(Was the Pharaoh of the 1st Dynasty)
  • Snefru(He built the first true Pyramid of Egypt)
  • Pepi II(He was Egypt's longest ruling Pharaoh with 94 years)
  • Ramses II(One of the most powerful Pharaohs of Egypt)
  • Cleopatra VII(The last Pharaoh)
